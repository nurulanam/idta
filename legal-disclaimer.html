2. Nature of the IDP

An International Driving Permit is a translation of your existing, valid driver's license. It does not grant driving privileges on its own and must be carried together with your original license at all times.

3. Acceptance Varies by Destination

Acceptance of an IDP is determined solely by the local authorities, car rental agencies, and law enforcement of the country you are visiting. While our IDPs are formatted to internationally recognized standards, iDTA cannot guarantee acceptance in every circumstance.
