Driving in Your Destination

Your International Driving Permit acts as an official translation of your home license, recognized by local authorities, police, and car rental agencies — so you can hit the road with confidence, without any language barrier or paperwork surprises.

Always carry your IDP alongside your original, valid driver's license — it's a translation and companion document, not a replacement. Rules on required validity and accepted formats can vary, so double-check with your rental agency or local authorities if you're unsure before you set off.
